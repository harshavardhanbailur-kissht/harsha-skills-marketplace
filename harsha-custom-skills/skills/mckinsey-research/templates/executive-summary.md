# Executive Summary Template (Standalone)

Use this template when delivering ONLY an executive summary without a full report.
Follows McKinsey bold-bullet SCR format optimized for C-suite scanning.

---

## Executive Summary: [Topic]

**[Situation: Current state of [entity/market/technology] — 1 sentence establishing context]
[Complication: What changed, what broke, why act now — 1-2 sentences creating urgency]**

**[Finding 1 — most important conclusion as a full, specific sentence with quantitative anchor]**
- [Evidence] — Source: [attribution], [date] ([High/Moderate] confidence)
- [Implication] — what this means for the reader's decisions
- [Caveat] — condition under which this finding might not hold

**[Finding 2 — second most important conclusion with specific data]**
- [Evidence] — Source: [attribution], [date] ([confidence])
- [Implication]
- [Caveat]

**[Finding 3 — third most important conclusion with specific data]**
- [Evidence] — Source: [attribution], [date] ([confidence])
- [Implication]
- [Caveat]

**Recommendation: [Specific, sequenced action with expected outcome and timeline]**
- Step 1: [Action] by [date], targeting [metric]
- Step 2: [Action] with [resource requirement]
- If this recommendation fails: [fallback plan]

**What would change this recommendation:** [Specific condition or evidence]

---

### Scanning Verification
- [ ] Bold text alone tells complete story
- [ ] Executive can make decision from bold text only
- [ ] SCR ratio: ~30% situation, ~70% resolution
- [ ] All findings are MECE (no overlaps, no gaps)
- [ ] Fits on 1 page (2 pages max for 50+ page reports)
