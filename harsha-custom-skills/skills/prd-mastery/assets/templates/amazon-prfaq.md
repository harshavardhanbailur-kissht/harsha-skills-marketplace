## Meeting Process

When presenting this PR-FAQ:

1. **Distribute** document before meeting
2. **Silent reading** for 15-20 minutes at meeting start
3. **Discussion** begins with junior participants
4. **Senior leaders speak last** to avoid anchoring
5. **Capture** questions and concerns for iteration
6. **Decision**: Proceed, iterate, or decline
